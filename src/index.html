SeedSong

Infinite piano melodies from a single seed number.

Generate, play, tweak, share, and export — right in your browser.

Open the app No install. No sign-up. Works offline.

Seed-based generation

Every melody is determined by a single number. Same seed, same song — always. Share a seed and anyone hears your exact piece.

Playable piano

Click, touch, or use your keyboard to play along. Two octaves with real piano samples via the Web Audio API.

Genre presets

One-click presets for Lo-fi, Jazz, Classical, Blues, Dark, and Funk — instantly set the right tonic, scale, tempo, and feel.

MIDI + WAV export

Download your melody as a MIDI file to import into any DAW, or render to a WAV audio file — all client-side.

Live visualizer

Piano keys glow as notes play, and a piano-roll score shows melody and chord structure in real time.

Zero dependencies

Pure vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no bundlers, no npm. Open source and easy to understand, fork, or extend.
